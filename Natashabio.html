RaceHer Profile: Natasha Chang


Chang celebrating after receiving the Guiness World Record

Natasha Chang is a melting pot of racing goodness in an island girl. Chang is a Race Car Driver,

Guiness World Record Holder, and Car enthusiast American born to a Chinese-Jamaican father

and a Puerto Rican mother, and raised in Jamaica.


Probably one of the best/most memorable days of her life would be March 14th 2019, when she won the

Guiness World Record for the Fastest Ascend up Doi Chang Mountain road by car in 6:52m.
