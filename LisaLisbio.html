RaceHer Profile: Lisa Abraham


LisaLis' Miss Behavin'

Lisa Abraham is a certified cosmetologist and owner of her own salon, The Racing Beautician.


She has always been actively involved in motorsports, and has served as

President of the Antigua & Barbuda Drag Racing Association.

She has also represented Antigua & Barbuda internationally.
