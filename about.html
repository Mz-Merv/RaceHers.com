About RaceHers


Heeyyy! You made it over here, so let’s tell you about RaceHers.com!


RacerHers was born out of the idea of creating a space to showcase the talents and accomplishments

of our Caribbean women involved in the world of Motorsports.


With the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in mind,

RaceHers ties neatly into SDG 5 - Gender Equality - as the world of Motorsports is dominated by men.


While we give you an insight into their lives, we'll teach you a bit about racing, the terms and car culture all together.



Strap in! Sink your clutch! And Goodspeed!


Oh! While we have you here, if you know of any other RaceHer doing her thing

who isn’t featured here, let us know at racehers@gmail.com
